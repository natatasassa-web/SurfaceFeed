# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Primarily Hinata — checked as a daily morning ritual. Designed as if shareable with a small audience of peers who value early signal over noise. The reader arrives with intention: they want to know what's converging before it goes mainstream, and they expect the experience to feel worth opening.

## Product Purpose

SurfaceFeed surfaces emerging narratives by detecting cross-community convergence across Reddit. The key insight: if unrelated communities independently talk about the same thing, that's signal. The product delivers that signal as a ranked, momentum-tracked digest — easy to parse at a glance, beautiful enough to linger in. It should feel like a considered daily publication, not a utility.

## Brand Personality

Considered, concise, meditative — with enough weight to feel intentional rather than precious. Quiet confidence, not minimalism for its own sake. A subtle retro sensibility woven through without tipping into nostalgia: the texture of print, the calm of a well-laid page, the precision of something made carefully.

**References:**
- july.fund — breathing room in spacing, calm visual rhythm
- collletttivo.it — uplifting energy and compositional confidence (not the gradients)
- timespent.so — button style; subtle retro detailing executed with restraint

**Anti-references:**
- amie.so — too plain, no personality
- usedropshot.com — too dark, too heavy

## Design Principles

1. **Worth opening.** Every session starts with a moment of arrival. The design should reward it — not with flash, but with the quiet satisfaction of something well made.
2. **Signal over noise.** Hierarchy is everything. The most important signal is always the most visually prominent. Decoration never competes with content.
3. **Quiet confidence.** Restraint with intention. Every element earns its place. Nothing is there to look designed.
4. **Retro without nostalgia.** Textural references to print and analog craft — in spacing, type weight, subtle material cues — without pastiche or period cosplay.
5. **Publication-grade craft.** This is a personal tool built to the standard of something shareable. The gap between "internal" and "polished" should not exist.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA standard. Strong contrast ratios, readable type at a glance, no motion that can't be reduced. Color is used for meaning (momentum states) — ensure each state is distinguishable beyond color alone.
